Composition

Composition is a design project based on up-cyccling.
The raw material for the construction
is a finished assembly of recycled wood scraps.

More details

This project looks at
how we use "clean" wood.
It would be interesting
to re-examine our perception of raw materials,
in this case recycled materials.
