At Home

"At Home" is a concept hotel
centred on the circularity of atoms.
The spaces proposed result from the crossing of atoms.
When they meet, they form a whole
and create the layout of the hotel room.

More details

"At'home" allows you to host meetings in your hotel room,
while separating the private and
intimate space of your bed from that of your business.
