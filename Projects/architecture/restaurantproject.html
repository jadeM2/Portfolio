Maylia

Maylia is a Mexican-Japanese fusion restaurant.
Come and enjoy your sensations seated at our tables
built into the floor.

More details

In the lower part of the restaurant,
the Japanese influence is reflected
in the use of tables with seats embedded in the floor:
customers slide in between the table and the seat.
The Mexican influence is found
in the upper part of the restaurant.
Warm colours are traditionally used in Mexico.
